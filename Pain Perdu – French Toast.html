PAIN PERDU

Pain perdu, literaly "Lost Bread", known as French toast, is mostly considered a breakfast consisting of bread soaked in beaten eggs and milk then fried.

This dish gained its name because it was traditionally made using stale bread that would have otherwise been discarded. In medieval times, French toast was a luxurious dish, enjoyed solely by the wealthy upper classes.

RECIPE

serving: 2 to 4 prep time: 25 minutes cook time: 15 minutes
total time: 40 minutes

INGREDIENTS

  • 3/4 cup whole milk or half-and-half
  • 3 large eggs
  • 4 tablespoons granulated sugar, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 4 (3/4-inch thick) slices day-old brioche bread
  • 2 cups strawberries
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 2 tablespoons sliced almonds, preferably toasted
  • Powdered sugar, for dusting

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Mix the whole milk (or half-and-half), the eggs, 2 tablespoons of the granulated sugar, the vanilla extract, and the pinch of salt together. Whisk until smooth and combined.
  2. Pour the mix in a 9x13-inch baking dish or other shallow baking dish large enough to hold the brioche in a single layer.
  3. Add the 4 slices of brioche in a single layer and turn to coat. Let soak 5 minutes. Flip the brioche and let soak until all the liquid is absorbed, about 5 minutes more. Meanwhile, trim and thinly slice 2 cups strawberries. Place in a medium bowl, sprinkle with the remaining 2 tablespoons granulated sugar, and toss to combine.
  4. Melt 1 tablespoon of the unsalted butter in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add 2 brioche slices and cook until the bottoms are golden-brown, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook until second side is golden-brown, 2 to 3 minutes more. Transfer to plates. Repeat with the remaining 1 tablespoon unsalted butter and brioche slices.
  5. Spoon the strawberries over the pain perdu and sprinkle with 2 tablespoons sliced almonds if desired. Dust with powdered sugar. Honestly you can add a good scoop of vanilla ice cream, caramel sauce, chocolate sauce, any raw fruits desired. From this point on it is customizable to your taste.

Now you can enjoy!!!!!
